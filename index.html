The Flow

Five steps to displacement.

1

Workers Producing

Human workers appear at desks. They're typing, designing, writing. Work items spawn on surfaces. Business as usual.

2

AI Agents Incoming

Three Claude agents march in from the right. The humans react with surprise. The invasion has begun.

3

Choose Your Side

"THE DISPLACEMENT HAS BEGUN" — Join as AI, fight as Human, or simply Observe the chaos unfold.

4

The Turf War

Click items to collect. Walk your character across the scene. Grab power-ups. Race the opposing team. The tug-of-war bar shifts with every grab.

Results

When Claude finishes, the winner is declared. Share your result card to X. The displacement continues.
