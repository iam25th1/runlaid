How It Works

Four steps to displacement.

1

Send Claude a Big Task

Write code, analyze data, draft a report. Anything that takes more than a few seconds.

2

Game Auto-Launches

After 5 seconds of streaming, a random game appears above Claude's input box. No setup needed.

3

Play While You Wait

Every game has different controls and mechanics. Quick intro tells you what to do. Progress syncs with Claude's response.

4

Results + Next Game

When Claude finishes, the game wraps up with your score. Next time, you get a completely different game.
